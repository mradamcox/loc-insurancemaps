Project Background →

1. Sign up!

Start by creating an account. Head back here once you are signed in.

If you don't want to sign up, no problem, you can still browse volumes, documents, layers, or web maps that other people have worked on.

2. Find a community you are interested in

Use the list on this page or head to volumes to find a set of maps to work on. You will find yourself at the volume summary.

3. Load the volume

In the volume summary you can load the map sheets into the database. Once loaded, you can begin processing each sheet individually.

If someone else has already loaded the volume, then just pick a sheet to start working with it.

4. Explore maps through georeferencing

In this application, the entire georeferencing process comprises three sequential steps:

  1. Preparation
  2. Georeferencing
  3. Trimming (optional)
In the volume summary all sheets will be sorted by which stage of the process they are ready for. You can pick one of these tasks and do it for many sheets, or follow a single sheet through the whole process.
