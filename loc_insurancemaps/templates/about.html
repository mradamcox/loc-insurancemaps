Purpose

This platform allows anyone who is interested to import and georeference scanned maps of from the Sanborn Maps Collection at the Library of Congress. Georeferenced layers can be trimmed and aggregated within public web maps to build seamless mosaics of historical Louisiana communities. This project is the basis of a M.S. Geography thesis at Louisiana State University.

Timeline

Study Period - February/March, 2022
This stage will see the heaviest use of the site, and users will be sent an optional survey near the end of March.

Data Transfer - April/May, 2022
At this point, single GeoTIFF files will be generated from the mosaics, and transferred to LSU Atlas where they will be published for for long-term public use.

Note: We are open to sponsorship, grants, or other partnership models to extend the scope and longevity of this project. Please email acox42@lsu.edu if this interests you or your organization.
