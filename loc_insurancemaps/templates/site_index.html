About this project

This platform allows users to import and georeference scanned maps from the Library of Congress Sanborn Maps Collection. Georeferenced layers can be trimmed and aggregated within public web maps to build seamless mosaics of historical Louisiana communities.

To learn how to participate, please visit the full documentation. Or, explore web maps that others have already created.

This "beta" release will be available through May 2022. At the end of this period, all georeferenced map layers will be transferred to LSU Atlas and made available for long-term public use.

We are open to sponsorship, grants, or other partnership models to extend the scope and longevity of this project. Please email acox42 [at] lsu.edu if this interests you or your organization.
